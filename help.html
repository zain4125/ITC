Help Center

If you need assistance with anything related to our services, you are in the right place. Below are some frequently asked questions (FAQs) to help guide you:

1. How do I register for an account?

To register, click on the "Registration" link in the menu and fill in the required information. Once completed, submit the form, and you will have access to your online banking account.

2. How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. You will receive instructions on how to reset your password via email.

3. What should I do if I have trouble with my account?

If you're experiencing issues, please contact our support team at +123 456 7890 or email us at support@mcb.com. We will assist you in resolving any issues as quickly as possible.
